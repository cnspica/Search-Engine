           <td class="answercell"> 
            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>This is kind of a play on words, but:</p> 
             <blockquote class="spoiler"> 
              <p> <strong>Options</strong><br> If you have 3 options, you have 3 options. If you have 2 options you have 2 options. If you have only 1 option, you don't have any options.</p> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>Along similar lines, this works too:</p> 
             <blockquote class="spoiler"> 
              <p> <strong>Choices</strong><br> If you have 3 choices, you have 3 choices. If you have 2 choices you have 2 choices. If you have only 1 choice, you don't have a choice.</p> 
             </blockquote>
