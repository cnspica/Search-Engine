            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p><strong>"gossiper"</strong> fits perfectly. (not specific for secrets, though)</p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <ul> 
               <li>a person given to gossiping and divulging personal information about others - <a href="http://www.thefreedictionary.com/gossiper">TFD</a></li> 
              </ul> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p><strong>"yenta"</strong> is a slang word for a person, especially a woman, who is considered to be meddlesome or gossipy.</p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <ul> 
               <li>a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others. e.g. "the couple's loud quarrel had the building's yentas yapping for a week." <a href="http://www.merriam-webster.com/thesaurus/yenta">MW</a></li> 
              </ul> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>related words: blab, gossip, gossipmonger, newsmonger, rumormonger, scandalmonger, tabby, talebearer, taleteller, tattle, tattler, tattletale, telltale, whisperer.</p>
