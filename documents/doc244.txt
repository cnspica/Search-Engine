        <p>Management is the process of getting activities completed efficiently and effectively with and through other people. The term Management has different meaning in different perspective.</p> 
        <p>This tutorial has been designed to give you a quick idea about most demanding managerial concepts in simple and easy steps.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>If you are an aspiring project manager or project leader, then definitely this tutorial is for you which will take you through almost all the important management concepts one by one using a simply easy learning approach.</p> 
        <h1>Prerequisites</h1> 
        <p>You do not need any prior project management experience to understand the given project management concepts.</p> 
        <hr> 
        <h2>1 - EVM Quick Reference Guide</h2> 
        <p>A quick guide to learn Earn Value Management.</p> 
        <p><a href="/earn_value_management/index.htm">Earn Value Management</a></p> 
        <h2>2 - PMP Exams Preparation</h2> 
        <p>Prepare for Project Management Professional - PMP Certification administered by the Project Management Institute (PMI).</p> 
        <p><a href="/pmp-exams/index.htm">PMP Exams Preparation</a></p> 
        <h2>3 - Understanding Six Sigma</h2> 
        <p>Six Sigma is a methodology for pursuing continuous improvement in customer satisfaction and profit.</p> 
        <p><a href="/six_sigma/index.htm">Six Sigma Guide</a></p> 
        <h2>4 - Understanding SEI CMMI</h2> 
        <p>SEI CMMI is a process improvement approach that provides organizations with the essential elements of effective processes.</p> 
        <p><a href="/cmmi/index.htm">SEI CMMI ® Tutorial</a></p>
