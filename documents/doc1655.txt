            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>In the <strong>mobile</strong> theme of main (non-meta) sites, <em>non-tag</em> badge names are sometimes unreadable: the font color is <code>#294586</code> while the background is <code>#333333</code>. Examples from Stack Overflow: </p> 
             <h3><a href="http://stackoverflow.com/help/badges/6/supporter">Individual badge page</a></h3> 
             <p><img src="http://i.stack.imgur.com/rWBWg.png" alt="badge page"></p> 
             <h3><a href="http://stackoverflow.com/help/badges">List of badges</a></h3> 
             <p><img src="http://i.stack.imgur.com/JcOq6.png" alt="badges"></p> 
             <p>(Those visited are shown in blue). </p> 
             <p>Seen in: </p> 
             <ul> 
              <li>Opera 30 on Android 4.4</li> 
              <li>Stock browser of Android 4.4 </li> 
              <li>Chrome 43 on Windows 7 (I know mobile-on-desktop is not supported, but this seems relevant, and was more convenient for screenshots.) </li> 
              <li>Mobile Safari on iOS 9.2.1</li> 
             </ul>
