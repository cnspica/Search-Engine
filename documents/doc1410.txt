     <div class="sectionheader"> 
      <h2>Reporting Security Vulnerabilities</h2> 
     </div> 
     <div class="sectionbody"> 
      <div class="sectionbody-full"> 
       <p> We take security vulnerabilities very seriously and work hard to provide a secure online experience for our users. If you believe you have discovered a security issue that affects many users, please report it to us so we can investigate and correct the problem. </p> 
       <p> To report a security vulnerability that affects many users, please fill out this form: </p> 
       <p> <a class="btn" href="http://stackoverflow.com/contact">Contact Form</a> </p> 
       <p> Choose "Other" as the type and include the words "Security Vulnerability". Be as detailed as you can in the steps required to reproduce the error. Please <em>do not</em> post serious security issues publicly on a meta site or elsewhere. </p> 
      </div>
