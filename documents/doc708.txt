           <td class="answercell"> 
            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>You can do whichever you prefer. You'll have to sign up for each site individually and there's no particular benefit to having an account on a site you don't participate on, so in my experience most people don't create an account on a site until they need to. But "need" can be anything from asking or answering a question to flagging something to favoriting a question. I once created an account because I wanted to bookmark (i.e. favorite) a question on their meta.</p> 
             <p>Accounts that have 1 reputation and no participation get automatically deleted after a while (six months? a year?). So if you create such accounts and never use them you mind find that they've gone away, but if so you can just recreate them. The association bonus protects you from this.</p>
