            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>There are multiple questions, <a href="/questions/tagged/feature-request" class="post-tag required-tag" title="show questions tagged 'feature-request'" rel="tag">feature-request</a>s specifically, on per-site metas that affect the whole network. Migrating these questions here would make it seen by a larger community, likely bring it to the attention to the powers that be, and allow it to be answered in a broader sense.</p> 
             <p><strong>My proposal:</strong> Just as every site has a default migration path to its per-site meta, every per-site meta should have a default migration path to Mother Meta, AKA Meta.SE.</p>
