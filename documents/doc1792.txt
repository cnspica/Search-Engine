      <h1 class="blog-title"><a href="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/2016/04/the-great-science-fictionfantasy-tv-showdown/">The Great Science-Fiction/Fantasy TV Showdown!</a></h1> 
      <div class="meta-info">
       <span class="postedDate">2016-04-25</span> by 
       <a href="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/author/rogue-jedi/" title="Posts by Rogue Jedi" rel="author">Rogue Jedi</a>. 
       <a href="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/2016/04/the-great-science-fictionfantasy-tv-showdown/#comments">4 comments</a> 
      </div> 
      <div class="post-text"> 
       <div class="tweetthis" style="text-align:left;">
        <p> <a target="_blank" rel="nofollow" class="tt" href="http://twitter.com/share?url=http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/2016/04/the-great-science-fictionfantasy-tv-showdown/&amp;text=The+Great+Science-Fiction%2FFantasy+TV+Showdown%21&amp;via=StackSciFi&amp;related=richardxthripp%2Ctweetthisplugin" title="Post to Twitter"><img class="nothumb" src="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/wp-content/plugins/tweet-this/icons/en/twitter/tt-twitter-micro3.png" alt="Post to Twitter"></a></p>
       </div>
       <p><a href="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/files/2016/04/HungerGames.jpg"><img class="  wp-image-2612 aligncenter" src="http://scifi.blogoverflow.com/files/2016/04/HungerGames-300x208.jpg" alt="HungerGames" width="343" height="238"></a></p> 
       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
       <p>If there’s one thing science-fiction and fantasy fans like more than watching, reading and playing science-fiction and fantasy, it’s <em>arguing</em> about watching, reading and playing science-fiction and fantasy. Marvel vs. DC. <em>Star Wars</em> vs. <em>Star Trek</em>. Classic Who vs. NuWho. These debates may very well rage on for the rest of time.</p> 
       <p>But now, The Science Fiction and Fantasy Stack Exchange is going to what they can to help settle these debates. Every month or so (hopefully), we’ll host a bracket, where all members of the site will be able to vote in order to determine the best of the science-fiction and fantasy best.</p> 
       <p>This month, we’re doing science fiction and fantasy TV shows. I, with help from other members of site, have selected 48 science fiction and fantasy TV shows from across the decades. These 48 shows were chosen by popularity and notability, and range the full spectrum of sub-genres and fan-bases. I have arranged them to hopefully prevent any round one steam-rolling.</p> 
       <p>So without further ado, arranged in no particular order for the primary bracket, the shows!</p> 
       <ol> 
        <li><p>Trek Yourself Before You Wreck Yourself: <em>Star Trek: The Original Series /</em> <em> Star Trek: The Next Generation</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Not Your Children’s’&nbsp;Cartoons: <em>Futurama /</em> <em> Rick and Morty</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Fun For All Ages: <em>Adventure Time /</em> <em> My Little Pony Friendship is Magic</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>The Family That Slays Together: <em>Buffy the Vampire Slayer /</em> <em> Angel</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Who Are You? Who, Who, Who, Who?: Classic Doctor Who / Nu-Who</p></li> 
        <li><p>The Element Of Surprise: <em>Avatar: The Last Airbender /</em> <em> The Legend of Korra</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Mysteries And Stuff: <em> LOST /</em> <em> X-Files</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Batman V Batman: Dawn of Batman: <em>Batman</em> (‘60s) / <em>Batman: The Animated Series</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>What A Twist!: <em>The Twilight Zone</em> (Original) / <em>The Outer Limits</em> (Original)</p></li> 
        <li><p>Jedi Did Not See That Coming: <em>Star Wars: The Clone Wars /</em> <em> Star Wars: Rebels</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Turtle Power!: <em>TMNT</em> (Original) &nbsp;/ <em>TMNT</em> (2012)</p></li> 
        <li><p>No Spoilers!: <em>Game of Thrones /</em> <em> The Walking Dead</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Space And Stuff: <em>Battlestar Galactica (2004) /</em> <em> Babylon 5</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>DC on CW: <em>Arrow /</em> <em> The Flash</em> (2014)</p></li> 
        <li><p>This IS A Place For A Hero: <em>The Spectacular Spider-Man /</em> <em> Justice League Unlimited</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Aussies and Brits: <em>Farscape /</em> <em> Red Dwarf</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Simply Marvelous: <em>Marvel’s Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D /</em> <em> Daredevil</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Currently Airing Fantasy: <em>Supernatural /</em> <em> Once Upon a Time</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>The Spin-Offs: <em>Stargate: SG-1 /</em> <em> The Sarah Connor Chronicles</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>The Classics: <em>The Incredible Hulk</em> (1978) / <em>The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy</em> (TV series)</p></li> 
        <li><p>Animation Across The Nation(s): <em>Transformers</em> (1984) / <em>Gravity Falls</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>Pretty Funny: <em>Sabrina the Teenage Witch /</em> <em> ALF</em></p></li> 
        <li><p>T<span style="line-height: 1.5">ime After Time: </span><em><span style="font-weight: 400">Quantum Leap / </span></em><em><span style="font-weight: 400">Torchwood</span></em></p></li> 
        <li><p>There Are Two Left Over And I Can’t Think Of Any Connection But They Both Start With F, So Let’s Go With That:<span style="line-height: 1.5"> </span><em style="line-height: 1.5">Firefly /<em><span style="font-weight: 400"> </span></em></em><em style="line-height: 1.5">Fringe</em></p></li> 
       </ol> 
       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
       <p>In addition to choosing the most popular series, there also a chance for <em>you to win.&nbsp;</em>During the first week of the event, everyone will have the opportunity to choose the show they think will be the most popular. After the winner is chosen, everyone who correctly guessed will go into a raffle, and a randomly chosen member will get a 100 Rep bounty.</p> 
       <p>&nbsp;</p> 
       <p>To vote and predict, just click on this <a href="http://meta.scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/9512/the-great-science-fiction-fantasy-tv-showdown-page-in-progress/9513#9513" target="_blank">link</a>!</p> 
       <p>&nbsp;</p>
