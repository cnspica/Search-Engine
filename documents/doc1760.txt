             <pre class="lang-sh prettyprint-override"><code>#!/bin/sh
# To check internet access after booting

ping -c 4 8.8.8.8
status=$?

if [ $status -eq 1 -o $status -eq 2 ]
then
    zenity --warning --text='&lt;span foreground="red" font="15"&gt;Unable to establish Internet connection&lt;/span&gt;' --title="Connection status"
fi
</code></pre> 
             <p>Save this as <strong><code>con_status.sh</code></strong> file in your home directory. </p> 
             <p>Provide execution permission by : <code>chmod a+x ~/con_status.sh</code></p> 
             <p>Now open <strong>Startup Applications</strong> --&gt; <kbd>Add</kbd></p> 
             <p>Give it any name, any comment.</p> 
             <p>Command field : <code>sleep 8 &amp;&amp; ~/con_status.sh</code>. Click <kbd>Add</kbd>.</p> 
             <p>That's all! </p>
