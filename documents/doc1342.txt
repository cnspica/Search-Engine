          <div class="bio"> 
           <p>I am <a href="http://stackoverflow.com/users/250725/psubsee2003?tab=questions">hobbyist programmer</a> for the most part, although I do write programs at work for my own use (my job used to occasionally involves working with massive piles of data and spreadsheets and comparing and/or extracting bits and pieces), so <a href="http://meta.stackoverflow.com/users/182513/psubsee2003?tab=answers">numerous</a> short programs I have written with the help of SO have saved my sanity.</p> 
           <p>By day now, I am a Project Manager in the Telecom world and don't get to do the fun stuff anymore, but I am a BSEE &amp; Telecommunications Engineer by trade and education, and the only formal programming training I have is in FORTRAN back in college. I'm self learned in C#, VBA, and a little VB6 mixed in, and my next goal is to try to learn Java.</p> 
           <p>I'm always looking to know more about C#, LINQ, XML, and XSD, and throw in some SQL as well.</p> 
           <p>In my personal life, aside from my precious time with my family, I enjoy golf in addition to the aforementioned programming, as well as new technology. I build computers once in a while and am running my own Home Theater PC.</p>
