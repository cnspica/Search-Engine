      <h1 class="blog-title"><a href="http://tex.blogoverflow.com/2015/08/registering-for-the-dante-autumn-meeting-2015/">Registering for the DANTE autumn meeting 2015</a></h1> 
      <div class="meta-info">
       <span class="postedDate">2015-08-15</span> by 
       <a href="http://tex.blogoverflow.com/author/stefankottwitz/" title="Posts by Stefan Kottwitz" rel="author">Stefan Kottwitz</a>. 
       <a href="http://tex.blogoverflow.com/2015/08/registering-for-the-dante-autumn-meeting-2015/#comments">0 comments</a> 
      </div> 
      <div class="post-text"> 
       <div class="tweetthis" style="text-align:left;">
        <p> <a target="_blank" rel="nofollow" class="tt" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Registering+for+the+DANTE+autumn+meeting+2015+http%3A%2F%2Fbit.ly%2F1hdUcQR+%23tex" title="Post to Twitter"><img class="nothumb" src="http://tex.blogoverflow.com/wp-content/plugins/tweet-this/icons/en/twitter/tt-twitter-micro4.png" alt="Post to Twitter"></a></p>
       </div>
       <p>I already wrote it on <a href="http://latex-community.org/">LaTeX-Community.org</a>: at <a class="postlink" href="http://www.dante.de/events/Herbst2015/anmeldung.html" rel="nofollow">http://www.dante.de/events/Herbst2015/anmeldung.html</a> you can now register to the autumn meeting 2015 of DANTE e.V. It takes place on September 5th at the TU Graz. Besides the member’s meeting and the program with talks, there will be an evening gathering the day before, and a tourist program afterwards. Early registering is extremely helpful for the organizers!</p> 
       <p>The meeting is also open for TeX friends who are not member of DANTE e.V. Participation at the meeting and at the talks is free.</p> 
       <p>I registered already, and I will fly Sep 4 from Hamburg to Graz and Sep 6 back. If anybody likes to join me, send me an email: I can get up to 2 cheaper tickets for traveling partners, because I work at the airline.</p> 
       <p>All information regarding place, travel information, and program can be found <a class="postlink" href="http://www.dante.de/events/Herbst2015.html" rel="nofollow">here</a>.</p> 
       <p>Stefan</p>
