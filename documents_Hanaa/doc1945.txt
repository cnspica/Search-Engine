        <h2>Slide</h2>

        <p><b>Package</b> : org.apache.poi.xslf.usermodel</p>

          <tr> 
           <td>2</td>

           <td>4</td> 
           <td><p><b>XSLFTheme getTheme()</b></p> <p>Returns the <b>XSLFTheme</b> object of the current slide.</p></td>
