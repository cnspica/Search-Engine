       <h2>Top Stats:</h2> 
       <p><strong>Top Question</strong></p> 
       <p><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/113497/if-luke-skywalker-didn-t-want-to-be-found-why-did-he-leave-a-map">If Luke Skywalker didn’t want to be found, why did he leave a map?</a> was asked by vs06, earning 132 votes, with the highest voted answer being from KutuluMike.</p> 
       <p> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/20142/vs06"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/20142.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/2816/kutulumike"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/2816.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> </p> 
       <p><strong>Top Answer</strong></p>

This answer beat out the accepted answer by David Banner by 93 votes.</p>

       <h2>User Favorites:</h2> 
       <p><span id="more-2585"></span></p> 
       <p> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/20774/richard"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/20774.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> </p> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Question</strong></p> 
       <p>I liked this one because, quite frankly, I had the same question in mind while I was watching the film</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/113497/if-luke-skywalker-didn-t-want-to-be-found-why-did-he-leave-a-map">If Luke Skywalker didn’t want to be found, why did he leave a map?</a></li> 
       </ul> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Answers</strong></p> 
       <p>Not to blow my own trumpet, but I thought my own answer to this question was nicely comprehensive</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/122275/was-kylo-rens-lightsaber-activated-by-the-character-that-it-then-killed">Was Kylo Ren’s lightsaber activated by the character that it then killed?</a></li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>as was my answer to this one</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/120122/why-did-the-dursleys-give-harry-glasses">Why did the Dursleys give Harry glasses?</a></li> 
       </ul> 
       <hr> 
       <p> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/51142/cheese"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/51142.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> </p> 
       <p>As a start, my favorite answer to one of my questions this quarter was <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/a/117720/51142">Does Gringotts employ any non-goblins?</a></p> 
       <p><em>Note, the linked answer has been deleted.</em></p> 
       <hr> 
       <p> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/21267/mooz"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/21267.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> </p> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Question</strong></p> 
       <p><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/116115/what-is-the-in-universe-title-of-yandels-a-world-of-ice-and-fire">What is the in-universe title of Yandel’s ‘A World of Ice and Fire’?</a></p> 
       <p>For attracting <a href="http://meta.scifi.stackexchange.com/a/8074/21267">yet another professional to our site</a>.</p> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Answer</strong></p> 
       <p>My own <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/a/117943/21267">answer</a> to <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/107687/how-many-people-actually-died-during-the-war-of-the-five-kings">How many people actually died during The War of the Five Kings?</a> because any excuse for me to dig deep into the ASOIAF lore is a joy for me.</p> 
       <hr> 
       <p> <a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/40294/praxis"> <img src="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/users/flair/40294.png" width="208" height="58" alt="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts" title="profile for enderland at Science Fiction &amp; Fantasy Stack Exchange, Q&amp;A for science fiction and fantasy enthusiasts"> </a> </p> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Questions</strong></p> 
       <p>In no particular order:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><strong>randal’thor’s</strong> question <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/114773/when-did-pure-historical-doctor-who-go-out-of-fashion">When did pure-historical <em>Doctor Who</em> go out of fashion?</a></strong> — I like questions that track trends in long-running science fiction works!</li> 
        <li><strong>N_Soong’s</strong> question <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/122654/are-any-regular-bridge-crew-on-tng-actually-members-of-the-next-generation">Are any regular bridge crew on <em>TNG</em> actually members of the next generation?</a></strong> — One of those questions that few would think about until an astute observer asks it!</li> 
        <li><strong>WadCheber’s</strong> question <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/116040/which-is-bigger-the-star-forge-or-the-death-star">Which is bigger: the Star Forge or the Death Star?</a></strong> — I’m partial to this one for several reasons: it’s an interesting question that tries to put two mega-structures in the <em>Star Wars</em> universe on some common ground, but I also like it because I answered it and enjoyed doing the calculations that resolved the question (and I netted a 500-point bounty in the process — thanks Wad!)</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p><strong>Favourite Answers</strong></p> 
       <p>Also in no particular order:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li><strong>Richard’s</strong> answer to <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/questions/122275/was-kylo-rens-lightsaber-activated-by-the-character-that-it-then-killed">Was Kylo Ren’s lightsaber activated by the character that it then killed?</a></strong> — I always enjoy an answer that locks down a question completely, with multiple references that leave no room for quibbling…</li> 
        <li><strong>DVK’s</strong> answer to <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/a/114250/40294">What does an astromech droid actually do while deployed in combat?</a></strong> — a comprehensive, model answer!</li> 
        <li><strong>Mazura’s</strong> amazing answer to <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/a/116904/40294">Where is Vader’s meditation chamber on the Executor?</a></strong> — such excellent work deserves more votes!</li> 
        <li><strong>Politank-Z’s</strong> answer to <strong><a href="http://scifi.stackexchange.com/a/116331/40294">Which Clark Gable movie did Edith Keeler want to see with Captain Kirk?</a></strong> — while not the accepted answer, it’s an enjoyable example of lateral thinking</li> 
       </ul>
