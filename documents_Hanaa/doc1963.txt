            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>Try <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/the_straw_that_broke_the_camel%27s_back">The straw that broke the camel's back</a></p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <p>It describes the small thing which causes&nbsp;failure, or causes inability or unwillingness to&nbsp;endure any more of something.&nbsp;</p> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>A <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Straw_that_broke_the_camel%27s_back">different interpretation</a> from Wikipedia. </p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <p>It describes the seemingly minor or routine action which causes an unpredictably large and sudden reaction, because of the cumulative effect of small actions.</p> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>There is also <strong>the last drop makes the cup run over</strong>. </p> 
             <p>Some other variants are also present, like <strong>the straw that breaks the horse's back."</strong> Feel intrigued to search them. </p>
