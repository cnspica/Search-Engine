        <p><b>Advanced Excel</b> is a comprehensive tutorial that provides a good insight into the latest and advanced features available in Microsoft Excel 2013. It has plenty of screenshots that explain how to use a particular feature, in a step-by-step manner.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>This tutorial has been designed for all those readers who depend heavily on MS-Excel to prepare charts, tables, and professional reports that involve complex data. It will help all those readers who use MS-Excel regularly to analyze data.</p> 
        <h1>Prerequisites</h1> 
        <p>The readers of this tutorial are expected to have a good prior understanding of the basic features available in Microsoft Excel.</p>
