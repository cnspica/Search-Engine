            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>I think most users will agree with me for this one. Sometimes a post make us curious and we want to learn more about it so we favourite it. But we don't receive any notifications in the inbox for those posts due to which we forget about it most of the times. We receive notifications for only those posts that we are more closely associated with. But won't it be better if we receive notifications for our favourite posts too. </p> 
             <p>Also while using SE via android app there's no way to go to your favourite posts. I think receiving notification for favourite posts will make the SE android app experience better.</p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <p><strong>I'm strictly talking about the android app experience</strong></p>

        }
    })();
