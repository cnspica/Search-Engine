            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>When hovering the "delete" link over my own post, I see:</p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <p><a href="http://i.stack.imgur.com/cxEKz.png" rel="nofollow"><img src="http://i.stack.imgur.com/cxEKz.png" alt=""></a></p> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>Text:</p> 
             <blockquote> 
              <p>vote to delete this post</p> 
             </blockquote> 
             <p>However, when deleting my own post, the vote is binding and causing instant deletion, so the "vote" part is confusing.</p> 
             <p>Three years ago, the alert itself <a href="http://meta.stackexchange.com/questions/172063/make-it-clear-a-self-delete-vote-isnt-a-vote-its-just-deleting">has been changed to "delete this post"</a>, can the same please by applied to the tooltip?</p>
