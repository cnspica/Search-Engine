       <p>These friendly <b>reputation leagues</b> are an informal way of tracking your reputation within the community on a particular Stack Exchange.</p> 
       <p>Reputation is capped at <b>200</b> per day, but remember that bounty awards and accepted answers are immune to this daily reputation cap.</p>
