            <div class="post-text" itemprop="text"> 
             <p>On Drupal Answers we just had a spam question (now deleted).</p> 
             <p>The question's title was, in its entirety:</p> 
             <pre><code>http://premiumpureforskolinrev.com/metabo-garcinia/
</code></pre> 
             <p>I can't think of a good reason that any reasonable question, on any site in the network, could consist of only a URL. </p> 
             <p>I think such questions should be blocked before they are asked; can this be implemented?</p> 
             <p>And another one today (a couple of months later):</p> 
             <p><a href="http://drupal.stackexchange.com/questions/162114/http-testosteroneboosterup-com-xtra-booster">http://drupal.stackexchange.com/questions/162114/http-testosteroneboosterup-com-xtra-booster</a></p> 
             <p>Over a year later and it's still going on:</p> 
             <p><a href="http://drupal.stackexchange.com/questions/200839/http-health-wellnessworld-com-zynev-male-enhancement-review">http://drupal.stackexchange.com/questions/200839/http-health-wellnessworld-com-zynev-male-enhancement-review</a></p> 
             <p>Any chance this could be looked at?</p>
