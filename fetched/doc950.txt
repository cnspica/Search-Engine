     <div id="sideBar"> 
      <div class="module newuser"> 
       <p> When your fellow users vote up your questions and answers on a Stack Exchange site, you generate <b>reputation</b>. Reputation is a rough measure of:</p> 
       <ul> 
        <li>how much the community trusts you</li> 
        <li>your communication skills</li> 
        <li>the quality and relevancy of your questions and answers</li> 
       </ul> 
       <p>These friendly <b>reputation leagues</b> are an informal way of tracking your reputation within the community on a particular Stack Exchange.</p> 
       <p>Reputation is capped at <b>200</b> per day, but remember that bounty awards and accepted answers are immune to this daily reputation cap.</p>
