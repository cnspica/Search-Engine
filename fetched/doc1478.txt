        <p>Interpersonal skills are sometimes referred to as <b>people skills</b> or <b>social skills,</b> and with good reason − these are skills that we use while communicating with others. Interpersonal skills include speaking, explaining, persuasion, and active listening. In the business world, it refers to a person's ability to communicate and interact effectively within the organization with colleagues and seniors.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>This tutorial is designed primarily for the students and professionals who seek assistance in initiating conversation and building positive impression in social circles and also at work.</p> 
        <h1>Prerequisites</h1> 
        <p>Before proceeding with this tutorial, you are expected to have a calm mindset and be open to exploring the suggestions mentioned here.</p>
