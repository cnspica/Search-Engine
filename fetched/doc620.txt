        <p>This tutorial provides a simple and practical way of understanding the essential areas of developing a website. It also provides elementary insights and an overview of the subject that is necessary to understand the world of web technologies. This tutorial covers all that is required to become a good Web Developer.</p> 
        <h1>Audience</h1> 
        <p>This tutorial is aimed at students who are interested in learning website development. It provides an in-depth coverage – starting from basics to Hosting concepts. In addition, it throws light on emerging concepts like E-commerce.</p>
