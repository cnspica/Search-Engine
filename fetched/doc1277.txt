          <div class="bio"> 
           <p>Last updated: 9 April 2016, after 3 Nov 2015</p> 
           <p><strong>A few things to know about me:</strong></p> 
           <ol> 
            <li><p>I'm a school student.</p></li> 
            <li><p>I sometimes write posts in a massive hurry, and often make careless mistakes.</p></li> 
            <li><p>I have two objectives on this site:</p> 
             <ul> 
              <li>Learn more interesting things.</li> 
              <li>Earn as much rep as possible. <em>I guess this objective has faded with time.</em></li> 
             </ul></li> 
            <li><p>I love this site (SE).</p></li> 
            <li><p>I'm waiting for the day this site makes a popular site worldwide, just like Wikipedia. I'm also waiting for the day 'Puzzling' becomes a full-fledged site (It's currently in Beta). <em>It seems that this will happen soon!</em></p></li> 
            <li><p>I offer bounties quite often. As of today <s>(3 Nov 2015)</s>, I have <s>2015</s> 2370 rep in my account and <s>1250</s> 1750 rep in offered bounties on Puzzling SE.</p></li> 
           </ol> 
           <p><strong>What I am known for:</strong></p> 
           <p>Simple questions which have no solution till date:</p> 
           <p><em>Check out the list at <a href="http://meta.puzzling.stackexchange.com/questions/4399/list-of-bounties-with-no-deadlines">List of bounties with no deadlines</a> It is updated and more reliable.</em></p>

            <li><em>Total rep = Earned rep</em>, not <em>Total rep = Earned rep - Spent rep</em>. Spent rep should be accounted separately so that the bounties you put equal the rep you have earned. For example, if you have earned 2000 and spent 400, your total rep is still 2000 and your leftover spending rep is 1600.</li> 
           </ol> 
           <p><s> 2. I'd like the process of creating a new site (Private and Public Beta) to be a bit faster. I can't understand one good reason why Puzzling SE should be called a 'beta' site. Does 'beta' mean there is something missing?</s></p>
