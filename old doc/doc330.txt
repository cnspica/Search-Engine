        <h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1> 
        <hr class="tall"> 
        <p>We come across various questions from our keen and enthusiastic readers. Here, we are answering few most important questions.</p> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Who started tutorialspoint.com? When? Who owns it?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>Tutorialspoint.com is a dedicated website to provide quality online education in the domains of Computer Science, Information Technology, Programming Languages, and other Engineering as well as Management subjects. This website was launched by an AMU alumni, Mr. Mohtashim, with a single tutorial on HTML in year 2006.</p> 
          <p>At present, this website is developed and maintained by Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd., which established on 12th June, 2014. (Twelfth day of June Two Thousand Fourteen), under the Companies Act, 2013. The CIN of the company is <b>“U80904AP2014PTC094598”</b></p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Where is Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd. located?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>We are located in Hyderabad, the beautiful city of Nizam. Our postal address is −</p> 
          <ul class="contact"> 
           <li><p><i class="icon icon-map-marker"></i> <strong>Postal Address:</strong> Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd., 388-A , Road no 22, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh-500033, <b>INDIA</b></p></li> 
           <li><p><i class="icon icon-envelope"></i> <strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:contact@tutorialspoint.com">contact@tutorialspoint.com</a></p></li> 
           <li><p><i class="icon icon-dribbble"></i> <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com">www.tutorialspoint.com</a></p></li> 
          </ul> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Can we rely on your tutorials and trainings in terms of their quality?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>To answer this, we would like to say that it’s our business to provide quality tutorials without charging a fee. We are striving hard to maintain the quality of our tutorials as high as possible. Our tutorials and training materials are being used by a lot of freelancer trainers and training companies as well to teach their students.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>What is main source of income for the website?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>The tutorialspoint.com is primarily funded by the founder of Tutorials Point (I) Pvt. Ltd. We also earn enough revenue from the advertisements published on the website and by selling ebooks. We are a financially strong company but we look forward to have trusted investors join hands with us and become a part of our mission.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Where is tutorialspoint.com hosted?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>Initially, tutorialspoint.com was hosted with Godaddy.com. In 2010, the website was transported with the most reliable hosting provider named Peer1, where we have setup more powerful servers to handle a large number of users.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>What is server configuration for tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>Currently tutorialspoint.com is running on a dedicated server.</p> 
          <p>Server Configuration -</p> 
          <p><b>Dual Xeon E5-2630 2.3 GHz Six -Core 12 Threads (w H/T)</b> server with <b>Kingston SSD</b> behind a <b>Juniper SSG 5</b> firewall.</p> 
          <p>Running on <b>Apache/2.2.15</b> on <b>CentOS release 6.5</b> and provided forum is supported by <b>phpBB-3</b></p> 
          <p><b>CMS - WordPress,</b> a powerful, reliable and simple CMS for managing short articles.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>What is current traffic on tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>At present, tutorialspoint.com is read by 13 million students and professionals around the world who access almost 35 million pages every month. The number of our users is multiplying every year.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Who writes the contents for tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>The content on the website are created by highly skilled professionals. A number of freelancers helped us in our effort to build a database of quality tutorials. They contributed their best tutorials into our Shared Tutorials Dictionary. We have established permanent setup in Hyderabad, INDIA, where we have a dedicated team of subject matters experts, technical writers, web developers and graphics designers who are taking the website to its next height of excellence.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>Can we reuse/publish the content available on tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>Sorry, we do not allow to use our content for republishing online or to be printed for commercial purpose. But yes, you can use our content with in your Schools, Colleges, Universities, Institutes and Companies as long as it is only for internal purpose and you are keeping appropriate references to tutorialspoint.com website.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>What is the mission of tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>Our mission is to deliver&nbsp;<b>Simply Easy Learning</b>&nbsp;with clear, crisp, and to-the-point content on a wide range of technical and non-technical subjects without any preconditions and impediments.</p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>How can I work for the website?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>If you wish to contribute, you are welcome. You can work with tutorialspoint.com as a freelancer, as well as a permanent employee at our office in Hyderabad-INDIA. To know about openings, visit the link − <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com/about/about_careers.htm">Careers at Tutorials Point</a></p> 
         </div> 
        </section> 
        <section class="toggle"> 
         <label>How can I help tutorialspoint.com?</label> 
         <div class="toggle-content"> 
          <p>It will be a great help to us if you can point out any typing errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect grammar used in any of the tutorials. Your contribution is extremely valuable and it will certainly help bring further improvement in our service.</p> 
          <p>You can send your findings/suggestions at − <a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com/about/contact_us.htm">Contact us</a></p> 
          <h2>Add a Link to Us</h2> 
          <p>To add a link, copy the following HTML code and paste it in the code of your website:</p> 
          <p>&lt;a href="http://www.tutorialspoint.com/"&gt;Tutorials Points&lt;/a&gt;</p> 
         </div> 
        </section>
